From humans, concerning humans, and for humans, studying Psychology during the last four years enables me to empathize with everyone around me.

As a fan of novel technology, my passion lies in researching, designing, and implementing both conventional online experiences and real-world living experiences for "users" of the future.

In my spare time, I capture photos of enchanting nature and charming cities. I love all kinds of tasty food and the experience of seeking delicious food in the city.

I am an interdisciplinary person full of random but distinctive ideas. I joined the MSI program hoping to gain skills and experience to shape me into a T-shape expert who not only ideates but also develops and makes those ideas come true.

During my undergraduate, I generally began with Java, then Data Structure using C++, System Programing using C, and finally wrapped up with Database System on MySQL, MongoDB, and leo4j. I have some project experience in Android apps and front-end development.

Regarding User Experience Research and Design, I am familiar with applying Design Thinking procedures to problem-solving and am handy in using prototyping tools like Figma.

It's a cliche, but I hope to create something that falls into that "sweat spot" crossed by desirability, viability, and feasibility, by researching, ideating, prototyping, and finally developing. I find my interests align with your application areas and hope to work with you!

Feel free to say hello to me at haofei@umich.edu!
